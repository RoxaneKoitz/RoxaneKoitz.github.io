Biography

I am a senior project assistant at the Faculty of Computer Science and Biomedical Engineering and Institute for Software Technology at Graz University of Technology.
My research interests are mainly in model-based diagnosis and abductive reasoning, but I have also done research on usability and human computer interaction for mobile devices. Have a look at my CV.
